## What it is

A Kanban board for a YouTube podcast production pipeline, plus a thumbnail + title approval flow on the right. Default view shows my real podcast guest **Steven Bartlett** with three production-quality thumbnail options. Click any other guest (Elon Musk, Naval Ravikant, Keanu Reeves, Sadhguru, Brené Brown, Sam Altman, Jim Gaffigan, and more) and the approval panel updates with hooks tailored to them.

The demo is populated with my **dream guest list** — people I'd love to interview on TwoSetAI someday. The Steven Bartlett thumbnails are real production assets generated by my actual thumbnail design skill.

## Why this workflow

In real life, every TwoSetAI episode goes through:

1. I generate plans + titles + thumbnails (markdown files) from a separate tool
2. I forward them to my producer, who approves in Slack
3. Approved ones go to my designer, who actually builds the thumbnail and uploads
4. Each guest is tracked in a scheduling spreadsheet

Three+ tools, multiple people, no single source of truth. This Kanban consolidates all six approvable items per guest (3 plans + 3 thumbnail+title combos) into one place with click-to-approve flow.
